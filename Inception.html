Inception

Rating: 8.7/10

Year: 2010

"Inception" is a mind-bending masterpiece that takes audiences on a thrilling journey into the realm of dreams and explores the complex layers of the human mind.

The film follows Dom Cobb (Leonardo DiCaprio), a skilled thief who specializes in extracting secrets from people's dreams. His mission involves inception — planting an idea in someone’s subconscious.

Overall, "Inception" combines a gripping concept, stellar performances, and stunning visuals, creating a cinematic experience that is both intellectually stimulating and emotionally resonant.
